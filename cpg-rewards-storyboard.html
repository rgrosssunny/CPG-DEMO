The member browses the same deals, then simply buys the product with their Sunny card. Filtered spend recognizes the purchase, auto-matches it to an active campaign, and credits the reward — no receipt, no scan. The member just gets an email confirming the cash back.

The takeaway

Same deals, two levels of effort: scan a receipt to earn, or let the card do it. Both pay to the same Sunny Rewards card.
